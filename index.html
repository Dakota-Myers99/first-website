About Us

We are conveniently located just off of Rt 56 in Sandyville. (NOT on the hill)
You can rest assured that your pet is in good hands as I have 20+ years of experience in animal care therefore your pet will receive the best of care!
We offer a 24 hour Live stream security camera mounted in the boarding area.
This allows our owners to check on their fur child not only anytime, but from anywhere.
We love offering this to our customers so they can always rest assured that their pet is being taken care of and is safe.
Weekend and evening grooming appointments available as well. Grooming prices depends on the size of the dog.
Please call (304)786-9341 if any further information is needed.
*Ask about our discounts for military and first responders*
Your pet will have an indoor a climate controlled 5’x4’ kennel (not a crate) with access to roofed outdoor concrete 4x8 runs with secure 6ft fencing.
Any dog staying with us for over 7 days will receive a Free Bath/deshed and nail trim
(offer does not apply to those staying at a discounted rate)

Prices

Boarding is $15 daily per dog.
Nail trims are $5.
For anyone who chooses to board with us, if you leave us a review on our Facebook page,
you will receive $5 off per day on your next scheduled boarding.
(Please show your review at your next appointment so we dont miss out on your savings!)
